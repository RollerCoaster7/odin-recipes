Spaghetti con Gorgonzola

Italy at its best!

Super creamy pasta, not too heavy because we're gonna add some freshness with lemonjuice

The more pasta, the more difficult the portioning. So: Less is more; Serve it on a romantic evening with your loved one!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti; 300 g
    • recommendation: Buy De Cecco; if not available: Try "Vermicelli Nr. 8" from Barilla
  • Gorgonzola; 250 g
  • Cream; 100 ml
  • Lemonjuice; 50 ml
  • Zucchini; 100 g
  • Onion; 1 middle-sized
  • Garlic; 2 cloves
  • Chives; 100 g
  • Butter; 20 g
  • Salt
  • Pepper
