Spaghetti con Gorgonzola

Italy at its best!

Super creamy pasta, not too heavy because we're gonna add some freshness with lemonjuice

The more pasta, the more difficult the portioning. So: Less is more; Serve it on a romantic evening with your loved one!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti; 300 g
    • recommendation: Get De Cecco; if not available: Try "Vermicelli Nr. 8" from Barilla
  • Gorgonzola; 250 g
  • Cream; 100 ml
  • Lemonjuice; 50 ml
  • Zucchini; 100 g
  • Onion; 1 middle-sized
  • Garlic; 2 cloves
  • Chives; 100 g
  • Butter; 20 g
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Your Steps

  1. Saut√® the onion and garlic in a larger pan in the butter
  2. In a large pot bring bring to boil plantiful of water, add salt
  3. In the meantime, first add the lemonjuice to the sauce and then the cream
  4. Boil the sauce for just a moment, then put down the heat and add the gorgonzola
  5. Cook the sauce at a very low heatlevel for the rest of the preparation
  6. Put the Spaghetti in the water, wait until they are al dente
  7. Put the pasta into the pan with the sauce directly from the boiling water
  8. Add chives and pepper to your pasta. Enjoy!

Secret hint

Adding the pasta to the sauce directly from the pot has the effect, that there is still plenty of water on the pasta, which makes the sauce even creamier!
