Potatoes au Gratin

A French classic to make your day a happy day

If you're feeling down, just want to relax on a cozy autumn evening, or just love a full belly there's nothing better than this recipe!

For a perfect gratin use waxy, regional, new season potatoes from your local farmers market

Ingredients:

  • Waxy potatoes; 1,5 kg
  • Cream; 250 ml
    • Just use 100 ml cream and dilute it with water if you want to save some calories
  • Cheese of your choice; 250 g
  • Nutmeg; 1 tsp
  • Garlic; 2 cloves
  • Lemonjuice; 50 ml
  • Oregano; 3 tsp
  • Butter; 4 tsp
  • Pepper; 1 tsp
  • Salt

Your Steps

  1. Sautè the garlic with the butter in a small pot
  2. Add the cream, salt, pepper, nutmeg, lemonjuice and oregano
  3. Bring everything to boil for a few minutes
  4. In the meantime preheat the oven to 230°C over-underheating
  5. Cut the potatoes in slices as thin as possible
  6. Put the potatoes in an oven mold and add the sauce
  7. After 30 minutes in the oven, add the cheese to the gratin
  8. After another 30 minutes the gratin is ready. Enjoy!

Secret hint

Add 300 g radicchio to add some bitterness; perfect for cold winter days
